WHO WE ARE

Board of Designers, founded in 2015, is a group of highly talented architects and interior designers striving towards the same goal of giving life to every client's dream. We have a remarkable team of architects and interior designers who can put forward a wide range of aesthetics to the table by which we execute the requirements of the client irrespective of the style. As a multidisciplinary firm, we have dedicated departments for building a dream project with magnificent interiors within a designated budget, for both residential and commercial. We have hands on experience with projects that vary from budget homes to high profile commercial projects with luxe and unique designs.

Mission

At Board of Designers, our mission is to deliver innovative and personalized design solutions by blending creativity, functionality, and cost-efficiency. We aim to bring every client’s vision to life through a multidisciplinary approach, ensuring excellence in residential and commercial projects from concept to completion.

Vision

To be a leading force in architectural and interior design, transforming ideas into inspiring, functional spaces that reflect our clients' aspirations—regardless of scale, style, or budget.
